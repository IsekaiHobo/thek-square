Investing

Invest in Olongapo's K-culture landmark

A four-floor destination at the concept stage, ready for founding partners.

A ground-up K-culture destination

The K-Square is a commercial development at R7GJ+QXG, Magsaysay Dr, Olongapo City, Zambales, bringing Korean retail, beauty, food, and entertainment under one roof. In its concept and pre-construction phase, it gives early investors the chance to shape the project from the ground floor up.

The case for investing early

01

First to market

The K-Square is the first development in the Olongapo-Zambales corridor built entirely around Korean retail, beauty, food, and entertainment, a category with no direct local competitor.

02

Four independent revenue floors

Retail, wellness, food and beverage, and entertainment each operate as their own leasing floor, spreading income across four distinct categories instead of one.

03

Ground-floor entry pricing

The project is still in its concept and pre-construction phase. Investors and anchor tenants who commit now enter before construction pricing and the surrounding market catch up.

How your capital comes back

Years 1-3

Dividend phase

Monthly dividends paid from net rental income in proportion to your contracted equity stake.

Years 3-5

Repayment phase

Installment or lump-sum repayment of the investment principal completes capital recovery.

After year 5

Profit phase

Premium dividends continue for 5 more years after your principal is fully recovered.
