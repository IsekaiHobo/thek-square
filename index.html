A new landmark for Olongapo

The K-Square brings Korea's beauty, food, entertainment, and culture under one roof: a four-floor destination designed to anchor Magsaysay Drive. Currently in its concept and pre-construction phase, it is built on a vision shared between investors and brands ready to shape what comes next.
